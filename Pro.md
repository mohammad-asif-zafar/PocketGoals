# PocketGoals - Project Requirement Document

## 1. What to Build

PocketGoals is a personal finance management application built with Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP).
It helps users track their income, expenses, and financial goals across Android and iOS platforms.
The app provides a modern, intuitive interface for managing daily transactions and long-term
financial planning.

## 2. Targeted Users

- Individuals looking to track their daily spending.
- People saving for specific financial goals (e.g., vacation, emergency fund, new car).
- Users who want to visualize their financial health through analytics.
- Multi-device users who need their data synced across Android and iOS (KMP).

## 3. Features

- **Authentication**: Secure Login and Signup flow with onboarding.
- **Dashboard**: Overview of current balance, recent transactions, and goal progress.
- **Goal Management**: Create, track, and manage financial goals with categories and timelines.
- **Transaction Tracking**: Easy entry for daily expenses and income.
- **Analytics**: Visual representation of spending patterns and goal achievement over time.
- **Profile Management**: User profile customization and settings.
- **Cross-Platform**: Consistent experience on both Android and iOS using Compose Multiplatform.
